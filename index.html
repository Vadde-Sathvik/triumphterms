Triumph Taekwondo Academy Summer Camp 2025 :

Dates : May 10th to May 15th, 2025

Location : 2nd Floor ,Plot No 354,Lane Number 13, HT Rd, Panchsheel Enclave, Alwal.

Terms and Conditions:

1. Eligibility and Enrollment

- Participants must be between [above 8years only] and registered members of Triumph Taekwondo Academy unless otherwise approved.

- All participants must submit a completed registration form and waiver before attending.

2.Camp Fees and Refund Policy

- Full payment is due upon registration.

- No refunds will be issued after May 1st, 2025.

- Refunds (less processing fees) may be considered before this date under exceptional circumstances.

3.Conduct and Discipline

- All campers are expected to demonstrate respectful behavior toward instructors, staff, and fellow campers.

- Bullying, violence, or disrespectful conduct will result in immediate dismissal without a refund.

- All camp rules must be followed as instructed by camp leaders.

4.Health and Safety

- Parents/guardians must notify the Academy of any medical conditions, allergies, or medications.

- In the case of a medical emergency, the Academy reserves the right to seek emergency basic medical treatment.

- All campers must have their own health insurance coverage.

5.Attendance and Punctuality

- Camp activities will begin promptly each day. Late arrivals may miss key portions of training.

- Parents/guardians must ensure campers are dropped off and picked up on time.

6.Uniform and Equipment

- All participants must wear a clean Taekwondo uniform (dobok) and bring appropriate sparring gear.

- Personal belongings must be labeled. Triumph Taekwondo is not responsible for lost items.

7.Media Release

- By attending, participants and their guardians consent to the use of camp photos/videos for promotional

purposes unless a written request for exclusion is submitted.

8.Liability

- Triumph Taekwondo Academy is not liable for any personal injury or property damage incurred during the camp.

9.Cancellation by the Academy

- The Academy reserves the right to cancel or reschedule the camp due to unforeseen circumstances.

-In such cases, participants will be offered a full refund or credit toward future programs.

10)Accommodation:

No Accommodation provide.
